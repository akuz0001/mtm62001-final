About Us

I'm Elsie, a 20-year-old creative with a passion for colors, design, and bringing ideas to life. Ever since I can remember, I've been captivated by the power of design to tell stories and evoke emotions. My journey into the world of design started with doodles in the margins of my school notebooks and evolved into creating websites for fun.
